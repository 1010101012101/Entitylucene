iTunes Search for a Song X Many Minutes Long <body> Can I search for a song in my music library that is between x and y start and end times, respectively?  <answer197769> This is only possible by creating a smart playlist.  Below the playlist sidebar click on the plus-button and choose to add a new smart playlist.
