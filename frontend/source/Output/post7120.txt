iPhone application compatibility <body> Does the Nike iPhone Sport Kit will work on a 2nd generation iPhone that has the 3.0 update applied? In the Q&amp;A on the website, some say it will, but others say it only works on a 3GS iPhone.  <answer7121> The Nike iPhone Sport Kit only works on the iPhone 3GS and later.  It won't work on the first iPhone or on the iPhone 3G.
