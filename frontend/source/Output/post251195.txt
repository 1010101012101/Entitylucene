Change the quote message heading in Mail <body> When you reply to an email the Mail app quotes the original message. The app increases the quote level and adds a heading like this one:     On 31 Aug 2016, at 19:34, X X &lt;x.x@example.org&gt; wrote:   Is it possible to modify this template somewhere?
