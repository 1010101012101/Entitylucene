cards.  One is integrated with the motherboard ( the Intel HD Graphics 4000) and one is a separate desecrate card that is connected to the motherboard (the NVIDIA GeForce GT 650M.)  The Intel "card" is a low power graphics controller that is good for most display tasks, while the NVIDIA card is a more powerful card that gets used by more demanding applications (like Photoshop, VMware, etc)  It looks to me like you are suffering from a GPU panic on the NVIDIA card, perhaps when the computer switches from one card to the other - or due to the card overheating or hitting a bad memory segment.  I am not excellent at reading these crash reports.
