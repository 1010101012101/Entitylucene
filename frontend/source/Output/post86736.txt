How can I transfer messages from iPhone 4 to iPhone 5? <body> How can I transfer my messages from an iPhone 4 to the new iPhone 5 that has a different sim card?  <answer86737> The only solution which I know is to make an backup (with iTunes or iCloud) from your iPhone 4 and use this backup to restore your new iPhone 5.  <answer86738> You can backup to iCloud, and then restore to your new phone.
