Mapping does not work with some characters in KeyRemap4Macbook <body> I want to map codeOption+Dot/code and codeOption+Comma/code to word-by-word movement in KeyRemap4Macbook. codeOption+Dot/code works but codeOption+Comma/code does not. Also using any other character such as W, V etc. instead of Comma does not work. They simply put the symbols normally mapped in keylayout file.  Here are the mapping items:  precode&lt;item&gt;     &lt;name&gt;word movement right&lt;/name&gt;     &lt;identifier&gt;private.wordright&lt;/identifier&gt;     &lt;autogen&gt;         --KeyToKey--         KeyCode::COMMA,ModifierFlag::OPTION_L,         KeyCode::CURSOR_RIGHT,ModifierFlag::OPTION_L     &lt;/autogen&gt; &lt;/item&gt; &lt;item&gt;     &lt;name&gt;word movement by option + dot&lt;/name&gt;     &lt;identifier&gt;private.wordmovement&lt;/identifier&gt;     &lt;autogen&gt;         --KeyToKey--         KeyCode::DOT,ModifierFlag::OPTION_L,         KeyCode::CURSOR_LEFT,ModifierFlag::OPTION_L     &lt;/autogen&gt; &lt;/item&gt; /code/pre  <answer130194> I found the cause of the problem. I have Turkish keyboard layout installed. The place of character codecomma/code corresponds to the place of character codeB/code in Turkish keyboard layout. The KeyCode definitions requires the character in the US keyboard layout.
