I'm backing-up to a Drobo FS  <answer31428> You can use timedog to see what's being backed up each time.   Usage:  ul liOpen Terminal (in /Applications/Utilities)/li licodecd /Volumes/Time\ Machine/Backups.backupdb/[Computer Name]/code Note Time Machine may be different on your system. Replace code[Computer Name]/code with the name of your computer (see System Preferences = Sharing)./li licode/path/to/timedog -d 5 -l/code For instance, if you unzipped timedog to your Desktop, the path would be code~/Desktop/timedog/code/li /ul  And then look at the output  <answer31453> Use  precodetmutil listbackups /code/pre  to get a list of existing (completed) backups.
