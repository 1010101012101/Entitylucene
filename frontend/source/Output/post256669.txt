How can I stop using beta iOS on my iPhone? <body> I'm currently on the beta program on my iPhone. How can I stop using the beta versions and go back to the newest version of iOS (non-beta)?  <answer256672> To opt out of getting future betas, delete the iOS beta profile. On iOS 10, this can be done as follows: Settings  General  Profile  Delete Profile.  This will not remove the installed beta. It appears, the easiest way you can go back to a non-beta version is by restoring a backup.   Some articles that may be useful:   ul liRemoving iOS beta profile/li liDowngrading to iOS 9/li /ul
