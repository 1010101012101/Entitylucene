Can't sync smart playlists that contains podcasts <body> I have a smart playlist that pulls the latest podcasts, however when I try to sync the playlist to my Shuffle 4th gen, nothing happens. Smart playlists with "normal" audio files, syncs fine.  Anyone know how to troubleshoot this?  <answer44050> If the playlist sync over but the podcasts fail to play, it could be that you have your iPod shuffle set to "shuffle songs". You must switch to "play in order" for it to work.  From Apple Support:     strongPlaying Audiobooks and Podcasts on iPod shuffle/strong      When using iPod shuffle (1st and 2nd generation), if iPod shuffle is set to strongshuffle songs/strong img src="https://km.support.apple.com/library/APPLE/APPLECARE_ALLGEOS/HT1831/shuffle_icon.jpg" alt="" it will play all the audio files on iPod shuffle except audiobooks and podcasts. To play an audiobook or podcast, set the switch on iPod shuffle to strongplay in order/strong img src="https://km.support.apple.com/library/APPLE/APPLECARE_ALLGEOS/HT1831/playinorder_icon.jpg" alt="".      When using iPod shuffle (3rd generation), podcasts and audiobooks will not be played when content is played from strongAll Songs/strong. If content is played from a playlist synced from iTunes, audiobooks and podcasts will only be played if the iPod's switch is set to strongplay in order/strong img src="https://km.support.apple.com/library/APPLE/APPLECARE_ALLGEOS/HT1831/playinorder_icon.jpg" alt="". In addition to playlists synced from iTunes, iPod shuffle (3rd generation) will create a special playlist for each audiobook synced and one for podcasts. When accessing these special playlists, audiobook and podcast content will play whether the iPod is set to strongplay in order/strong img src="https://km.support.apple.com/library/APPLE/APPLECARE_ALLGEOS/HT1831/playinorder_icon.jpg" alt="" or strongshuffle songs/strong img src="https://km.support.apple.com/library/APPLE/APPLECARE_ALLGEOS/HT1831/shuffle_icon.jpg" alt="". Podcast are sorted by alphabetical order and then by episode date.
