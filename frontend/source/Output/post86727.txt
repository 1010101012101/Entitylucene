What is the USB plug on the Mini-Display Port to Dual-DVI adapter used for? <body> http://store.apple.com/us/product/MB571Z/A/mini-displayport-to-dual-link-dvi-adapter  What is the USB plug used for?  <answer86730> The Dual Link DVI connector is an active adapter, and requires a power source (which Mini-DP cannot supply).
