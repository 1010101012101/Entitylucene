What's going wrong here?  <answer250615> From docs:  Pastes (both regular and slow) are done by splitting the text to paste into chunks. There is a delay between the transmission of each chunk. To change the speed that "paste" pastes at:  precodedefaults write com.googlecode.iterm2 QuickPasteBytesPerCall -int 1024 defaults write com.googlecode.iterm2 QuickPasteDelayBetweenCalls -float 0.01 /code/pre  To change the speed that "paste slowly" pastes at:  precodedefaults write com.googlecode.iterm2 SlowPasteBytesPerCall -int 16 defaults write com.googlecode.iterm2 SlowPasteDelayBetweenCalls -float 0.125 /code/pre  <comment195719> I suspect your paste speed may be too high.
