Date and Time imprints on photos taken using iPhone 6 <body> Is it possible to imprint date and time on the photos taken using iPhone ?  <answer171962> There are a number of apps that will do this, such as Timestamp It, TimeStamp Photo and Moment Diary, the latter of which is free.  The included Camera app does not have this functionality.  <answer235433> Jambula is a free and open source product that can batch insert shooting date/time/comment on a jpeg image in different formats and languages. A special feature is that the date stamp is lossless.
