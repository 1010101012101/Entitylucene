Is there a setting to force Adium to open a new chat in a new window? <body> For Adium, a single window holds many chats.  from official link (http://trac.adium.im/wiki/TabbedMessaging)  "A single window might contain 10 different tabbed chats"  Is there any setting where I can force Adium to open new chat in new window?  <answer44380> Yes.
