Toggle Ink's WriteAnywhere with AppleScript <body> How can you easily toggle the Ink preference pane setting "Write Anywhere"?  <answer8792> With this script you can toggle the Write Anywhere setting on and off, however it opens the System Preferences in the background:  precodetell application "System Preferences"     reveal (pane id "com.apple.preference.ink") end tell tell application "System Events"             tell application process "System Preferences"                 click checkbox "Allow me to Ink in any application" of group 1 of tab group 1 of window "Ink"             end tell end tell /code/pre  I use it for toggling WriteAnywhere from the "Radial Menu" of my Wacom tablet.
