Shortcut to open/close sidebar in Notes app? <body> In Safari, you can press kbd Cmd ⌘ /kbdkbd ⇧ /kbdkbd L /kbd to open/close the sidebar. When pressing this shortcut in Notes however, it creates a list.  What's the shortcut in Apple's notes app to toggle the sidebar?  <answer269851> They don't actually call it a sidebar, they term it 'Folders'  It's in the View menu as Show or Hide Folders, with no default shortcut  You could make your own shortcut in System Prefs  Keyboard  Shortcuts App Shortcuts - though you'd actually have to make two, for the different wording.br If you chose to re-use kbd Cmd ⌘ /kbd kbd Shift ⇧ /kbd kbd L /kbd you would also have to reassign Checklist from the Format menu to something else before it will work.
