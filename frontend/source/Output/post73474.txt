Making the Dock immutable <body> A relative who has difficulty with mouse control keeps inadvertently removing items from his Dock.  I enabled Parental Controls to prevent him from doing so, but Parental Controls has a bug that prevents access to secure websites, including any banking site.  How do I lock the Dock so that it cannot be modified, without making use of Parental Controls?  <answer73475> I found an answer on Apple Support Communities:  precodedefaults write com.apple.dock contents-immutable -bool true; killall Dock /code/pre
