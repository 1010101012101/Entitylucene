One possible solution is to just enable logging. You can edit:  precode/usr/libexec/ntpd-wrapper /code/pre  And add this (or something similar):  precode-l /tmp/ntpd.log /code/pre  To the codeexec /usr/sbin/ntpd/code line in the script (the last line). Then kill the ntpd process (it will automatically relaunch) or reboot.
