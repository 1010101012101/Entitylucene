How can I delete the "Recents" pictures when selecting a profile photo? <body> How can I delete the photos within the recent section? I tried pressing the delete button, but it didn't delete the image from the list.  img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/DBpWo.jpg" alt="Screenshot of issue."  <answer294012> The process is a bit complicated.  To delete the Photos in recent, you need to first navigate over to:  precode~/Library/Containers/com.apple.ImageKit.RecentPictureService/Data/Library/Images/Recent Pictures /code/pre  (strongFinder/strong  strongGo/strong  strongGo to Folder/strong)  There you will find all your recent pictures. However, you can't just delete those pictures. You have to also delete it from the plist file available at:  precode~/Library/Containers/com.apple.ImageKit.RecentPictureService/Data/Library/Images/Recent Pictures/Recents.plist /code/pre  Just open it in Xcode or any text editor, and delete the dictionaries under the array.  A picture in the plist will look something like this:  precode&lt;dict&gt;     &lt;key&gt;cropInfo&lt;/key&gt;     &lt;dict&gt;         &lt;key&gt;cropViewInfo&lt;/key&gt;         &lt;dict&gt;             &lt;key&gt;PRS&lt;/key&gt;             &lt;dict&gt;                 &lt;key&gt;px&lt;/key&gt;                 &lt;real&gt;0.0&lt;/real&gt;                 &lt;key&gt;py&lt;/key&gt;                 &lt;real&gt;0.0&lt;/real&gt;                 &lt;key&gt;r&lt;/key&gt;                 &lt;real&gt;0.0&lt;/real&gt;                 &lt;key&gt;sh&lt;/key&gt;                 &lt;real&gt;0.390625&lt;/real&gt;                 &lt;key&gt;sw&lt;/key&gt;                 &lt;real&gt;0.390625&lt;/real&gt;             &lt;/dict&gt;             &lt;key&gt;cropViewSize.height&lt;/key&gt;             &lt;real&gt;200&lt;/real&gt;             &lt;key&gt;cropViewSize.width&lt;/key&gt;             &lt;real&gt;200&lt;/real&gt;             &lt;key&gt;zoomValue&lt;/key&gt;             &lt;real&gt;0.0&lt;/real&gt;         &lt;/dict&gt;     &lt;/dict&gt;     &lt;key&gt;cropSize&lt;/key&gt;     &lt;string&gt;{200, 200}&lt;/string&gt;     &lt;key&gt;original&lt;/key&gt;     &lt;string&gt;68937D05-204D-4AD8-A98F-2DB8E9EF945F.png&lt;/string&gt; &lt;/dict&gt; /code/pre  You can delete each dictionary/picture manually by looking at the string parameter.  <comment371052> working like a charm, Thank you! <comment371054> No problem, glad I could help :)
