Keyboard Shortcut to Fill Right in Numbers? <body> In Numbers, is there an equivalent keyboard shortcut to Excel's "Fill Right" kbdCtrl + R/kbd and "Fill Down" kbdCtrl + D/kbd?  <answer38147> Go to  precodeSystems Preferences &gt; Keyboard &gt; Keyboard Shortcuts &gt; + /code/pre  to add a shortcut, select or add Numbers as the Application, and put "Fill Down" (exact, no quotes) in the command spot and then assign your shortcut (kbdCtrl + D/kbd)
