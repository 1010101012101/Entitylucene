How do I remove the sync status icon from the menu bar after migrating to iCloud? <body> How do I remove the sync status icon from the menu bar after migrating to iCloud?  img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/WbTaA.png" alt="Pre-iCloud sync status setting"  Prior to iCloud the Sync pane of MobileMe preferences had a simple setting for this, but after migration it is gone.  <answer27986> Hold the Command key while dragging the icon out of the menu bar.  Also you could delete ~/Library/com.apple.systemuiserver.plist and log out and then back in again. emKilling SystemUIServer might also work to repaint the menu bar, but I prefer to log out when changing a plist that also has an associated lockfile./em
