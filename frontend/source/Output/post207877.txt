What is the Mac equivilent of ALT+PrintScreen? <body> I've just started using Delphi to create OSX programs. I'd like to be able to take a screenshot of just the currently active window on the Mac and save it to the clipboard. On Windows you press [Alt] + [PrtScn].   What is the equivilent on a Mac?  <answer207878> To take a screenshot of a window press: kbd⌘ cmd/kbd+kbd⇧ shift/kbd+kbd4/kbd and then press kbdSpacebar/kbd. With this hover the mouse over the window You want to take a screenshot of and press Left mouse/trackpad button.
