Thanks!  <answer196386> First of all, delete the duplicate rouser line from snmpd.conf, leaving just the "rouser rouser priv" line.  Second, note that you created your user with AES:  codesudo net-snmp-config --create-snmpv3-user -ro -a 12345678 -x 12345678 -X AES -A SHA rouser/code  But are trying to walk using DES:  codesnmpwalk -v 3 -u rouser -l authPriv -a MD5 -A 12345678 -x DES -X 12345678 localhost .1/code  That's just not going to work. Swtich to code-x AES/code in the snmpwalk, and there's a much better chance it will work. ;-)
