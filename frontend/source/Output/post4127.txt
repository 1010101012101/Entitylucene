Textmate: removing sidebar on the left <body> Is there a way to remove this sidebar from Textmate?br img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/g7FRk.png" alt="alt text"  <answer4128> From the strongView/strong menu, select strongGutter/strong, and click to uncheck all of the selected view options there, i.e. strongFoldings/strong, strongBookmarks/strong, strongLine Numbers/strong, and strongSoft Wrap Indicators/strong. Once all of the above are unchecked, the gutter will disappear.
