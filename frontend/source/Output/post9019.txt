Why can my Mac charge my iPad but other computers can't? <body> What do Macs do differently so its USB ports can charge my iPad, but if I plug into a USB port on my PC I get the "not charging" message?  <answer9020> Because the iPad needs more than the 500 mA that the USB 2.0 spec says computers need to provide.  Macwork has more information.  <answer9024> Apple's newer machines (and some newer PCs I believe) have got USB ports that are able to provide more current.
