What are the sharingd and sharedfilelistd processes? <body> What are the sharingd and sharedfilelistd and why are they running?  img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/51lFR.png" alt="enter image description here"  I'm on OSX El Capitan 10.11.6  <answer291097> In a nutshell, strongfsharedfileslistd/strong is a CoreServices agent that is used by applications to read/modify recent documents/servers/hosts (those that you see in Apple-Recent Items menu), Favourites/iCloud items/whatever that you see in the finder sidebar, Recent items per applications (e.g. those that appear in Save/Open dialogs) and such.  strongsharingd/strong is sharing daemon that enables AirDrop, Handoff, Instant Hotspot, Shared Computers, and Remote Disc in the Finder.
