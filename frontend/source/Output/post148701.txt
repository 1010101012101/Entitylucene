How to resize a table in Pages for Mac? <body> How do I resize a table in Pages to fit page?  I am using an iMac with 10.9.5   <answer148706> First select the table by clicking on the table-select circle in the upper left.  Once selected you can grab the right edge of the table at the center select point to adjust the width left and right.
