How to see the current playback speed in VLC? <body> On Windows, VLC's playback speed can be discovered on status bar, but the Mac version doesn't have a status bar. Where should I go to see the current playback speed?  <answer250868> On the Mac version of VLC, to see the playback speed, you have to click the Playback menu.
