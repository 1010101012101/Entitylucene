Photos sync from iphotos to iphone <body> I downloaded all my photos from iPhone to Macbook Pro, and now that I have a new iPhone, I want to download from my iPhoto's photo album to my iPhone.   How do I do that?   <answer177516> Connect your new iPhone to the MacBook, then in iTunes, click on the device and click on Photos to select what is copied to your phone. See detailed steps on Apple's website.
