How do I get LaunchBar to open the enclosing folder for a selected file? <body> After selecting a file or application in LaunchBar, how do I get it to show it in the Finder? (I'd like the equivalent of right-clicking on a file in a Spotlight search and selecting "Open Enclosing Folder" in the pop-up menu.)  <answer56255> Command-Return  Hold the Command key and tap the Return key  This will bring you to a Finder window, where the file/app is selected within the enclosing folder.
