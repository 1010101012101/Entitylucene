Beside the ones aforementioned, I use SimpleTagger which enables you to tag from Amazon (including cover) and rename your files from tags.  <answer157755> Tag &amp; Rename works on PlayOnMac. PlayOnMac is a Windows compatibility layer (but it's not a virtual machine). It's free and you don't need a Windows license either. First, you need to download and install PlayOnMac, then buy Tag &amp; Rename and install it from within PlayOnMac. After that, it just works like a usual app.  <answer157783>  I mostly use codemid3v2/code.
