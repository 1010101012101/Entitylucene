<answer157841> A kernel panic in most *nix operating system is very difficult to debug. You can get hints from here how to submit a bug report http://support.apple.com/en-us/ts3742 but if you read the tech notes in the end of the article it points hints to either the OS kernel or driver causing the system to crash. The "quickest" workaround is to revert to the previous working state which unfortunately in your case is Mavericks.
