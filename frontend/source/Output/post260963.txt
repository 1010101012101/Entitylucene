(I can quit multiple processes one-by-one, but not altogether)  I'm not trying to quit Activity Monitor, it's just an example.  <answer295979> This doesn't seem to be possible within the Activity Monitor.  However, it can be done from the Terminal like so, as long as the processes you want to quit have the same name.  To view running processes, in the Terminal  precode$ ps /code/pre  To end processes by name  precode$ pkill -9 "nameofprocess" /code/pre
