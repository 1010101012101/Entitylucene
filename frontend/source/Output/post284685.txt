How to you get the "Share with Google Drive" contextual menu option in Finder? <body> Google Drive is installed but there is no option "View with Google Drive" or "Share with Google Drive" when I Control-click / Right-click on an item in the Finder like my coworkers have. How do you enable them?   <answer284686> You need to enable the Finder extension Google Drive.   Open the contextual menu for any file, and click on Share and then More.   img src="https://i.imgur.com/jzWJOLB.png" title="source: imgur.com" /  That will bring you to the Extensions window.   Click on Finder and in the "Select extensions for customizing Finder" select Google Drive.   img src="https://i.imgur.com/GQGefQe.png" title="source: imgur.com" /  Now you should see the "View in Google Drive" and "Share with Google Drive" options when you Control-click / secondary click on files inside your Google Drive.  img src="https://i.imgur.com/ljIsgwh.png" title="source: imgur.com" /
