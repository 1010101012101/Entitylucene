How to tell Mac OS Messages.app to go online from the command line? <body> I observed that quite often some accounts on Messages.app (Mac OS Sierra) may become offline and I want to automate the reconnection when I connect to VPN.  So, the question is: what command line commands should I run in order to start Messages.app and restore connections. Note: Messages.app may already be opened but my account may be offline.  <answer258874> Here is the command for terminal:  precodeosascript -e 'tell application "Messages" to log in' /code/pre  <comment323738> Thanks, exactly what I was looking for. Tested and worked.
